How do Magnetic Lashes work?

Unlike glue on lashes, magnetic eyelashes have two strips or multiple pieces of lashes with teeny tiny magnets already attached.

  1. Pop the top row of lashes on top of your upper lashes.
  2. Place the bottom piece(s) below your upper lashes.
  3. The magnets will ping together around your natural lashes.
